| 01 : First Job |

I was working for “Telekom Romania at Business Service Center” (BSC) department as “Network Technical Support” for premium services provided to Telekom Corporate Clients. Among the most important responsibilities I had there were troubleshooting and fixing an important area of IT&C services the company offered to its Corporate Customers. Telekom services which were sold then were: Internet, Virtual Private Network, Voice, E-mail and Hosting Services, and other Integrated Solutions. The technologies that I've worked with were xDSL, FTTx, VPN MPLS, VPNoI, VoIP, SIP Trunk, GSM. My capabilities for that job were gathering the exact information from the client to start the troubleshooting, check the first layer together with the client (link and power problems), then the CPE if it's working and if it's well configured (Modem/Router), then the infrastructure equipment (BRAS, DSLAM, PE). If encountered a configuration or infrastructure problem the issue was forwarded to NOC or IT department.
